Requirement Description
The Client seeks to establish a comprehensive ride-booking platform by aggregating
ride providers and passengers, enabling seamless booking of rides via the application.
Objectives:
Optimize ride booking by matching passengers with nearby drivers in realtime. Enhance ride booking efficiency and reduce costs.
Stakeholders for this solution:
The platform will have following stakeholders:

Customers: Customers are the end users who will place the request for the taxi via
the platform.
Drivers: Drivers are the employees or the freelancers who will receive the request
and will perform the service accordingly.
Service Owner Admin: Service owner owning and managing the entire platform:
users, tasks, content and other key operations. Our solution will include native mobile application for the customers and drivers
(Driver App). An admin panel (Dashboard) for the service owner on our base
template to manage all stakeholders and other key operations.
Technical Solution
We will offer the design and development of an application for the client and help that
is flexible, user friendly and allows users to navigate through the key information
conveniently.
